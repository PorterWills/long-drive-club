The running order and the practical bits. Most of this firms up in the last fortnight. We'll fill it in as it does.

Breakfast from 06:30, coffee on. Something at the turn. Full timings land on the day sheet the week before.

Two players can share one car. Each applies, each pays, each takes one of the 20 places. Tell us who you're paired with when we send the sheet, so the field reads right.

Not bringing a car? You can still take a place. We'll pair you with a member who has room. You apply and play like anyone else.

There's a wash and quick detail laid on before the photos. Optional. Confirmed closer to the day.

We set off in waves of 4, 10 minutes apart. No convoy heroics, no one left at lights.

One planned stop on the way. From a full tank, most cars won't need it.
