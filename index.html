What this is

Some mornings the car is the whole reason you go.

Long Drive Club is for drivers who happen to play golf. A road chosen because it's worth driving. A course chosen because it's worth arriving at. And a field of people who get why those are the same sentence.

Three drives a year. This is the first.

Long Drive Club is a members' club for people who treat the drive and the round as one day, not two. We're at the start.
