# LDC Graded Lines

The full calibration corpus. Every line was graded by the founder. The verdict and the reason are the standard. Read this file whenever a judgement call is close, especially on the true / only-LDC / unforced / non-presuming boundaries.

Verdict key: KEEP holds the standard. SOFT is weak but survivable, treat the reason as a warning. CUT fails, never ship it.

## The patterns underneath the calls

Almost every CUT is one of four failures.

1. **Not true.** Off the line, roads kept yours, best part before the tee, three drives before they happened.
2. **Not only LDC.** Something else, everyone gets it, rather drive than fly, smallest detail, everything you need nothing you don't.
3. **Performed.** Third beat strain, slang reaching for cool, soft relationship warmth, cliché.
4. **Directs or presumes.** Fuel up, arrive on a full tank, you ready, everyone gets it.

Under those, the mechanical gate catches numbers as words, "and" after a comma, slang, compound hyphens.

When a new line is close, name which of the four it might be failing, then check the gate. If it passes all four and the gate, it ships.
